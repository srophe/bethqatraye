Syria — ܣܘܪܝܐ

https://bqgazetteer.bethmardutho.org/place/486
A historical region bounded on the north by the Euphrates River and the Taurus Mountains, on the west by the Mediterranean Sea, on the east by the desert, and on the south by Palestine, which during the medieval period was considered the southernmost district of Syria.
