Burṣ - بُرص

https://bqgazetteer.bethmardutho.org/place/5249
A village in al-ʿIrāq that is now “a ruined site 9 miles S.W. of the town of Ḥilla on the Euphrates, about 12 miles S.S.W. of Babylon on the eastern shore of the Lake of Hindiyya.” 2
