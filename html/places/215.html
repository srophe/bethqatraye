Emesa — ܚܡܨ

https://bqgazetteer.bethmardutho.org/place/215
A “town in Syria (36° E. and 34° 20′ N.) 500 m above sea level on the eastern bank of the Orontes (Nahr al-ʿĀṣī), in the centre of a vast cultivated plain which is bounded in the east by the desert and in the west by volcanic mountains.” 11
