al-Qulzum - القلزم

https://bqgazetteer.bethmardutho.org/place/5098
“[A]n ancient town and seaport on the Red Sea (A. Baḥr al-Ḳulzum [q.v.], Baḥr al-Hind or Baḥr al-Ḥabas̲h̲a), now administratively in the province (muḥāfaẓa) of al-Suways.” 2
