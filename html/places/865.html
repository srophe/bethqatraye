Samarqand

https://bqgazetteer.bethmardutho.org/place/865
“An ancient city of Transoxania, the Arabic Māʾ warāʾ al-Nahr [q.v.], situated on the southern bank of the Zarafs̲h̲ān river or Nahr Ṣug̲h̲d.” 5
